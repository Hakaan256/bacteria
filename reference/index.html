Goal:
Create the strongest bacteria possible.

Bacteria:
Resistance designated by darkness of border.
    [black = very reistant, white = weakly resistant]
Health designated by darkness of center. Bacteria will heal over time.
    [black = weak, purple = healthy]
Bacteria placed with swab will be of random antibiotic resistance.

Spawning:
If nearby other bacteria, will likely spawn.
If no bacteria is nearby, very small chance to asexually spawn.
If food nearby, will very likely spawn another bacteria onto the food.
Spawned bacteria will have a resistance that is the average of its parents
    [in the case of asexual reproduction, will produce a clone]
Exception- any spawn has a small chance of having a random, unpredictable mutation with respect to its antibiotic resistance.

Antibiotics:
Strength designated by darkness of border.
    [black = strong, white = weak]
Qty left designated by darkness of center.
    [black = full qty, white = almost gone]

Food:
Encourages bacteria spawning.
All food is identical.
